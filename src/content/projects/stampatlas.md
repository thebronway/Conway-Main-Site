# A Self-Hosted Passport Travel App

> **Note:** This project is its initial planning phase

## Purpose
A mobile-first web application designed for tracking travel experiences through digital Passports. The goal of the self-hosted version is to provide a private, local tool for travel enthusiasts to own and track their travel data without the noise of social media features.  Passports aren't just limited to regions like states or countries, but they are desgined to track just about anything.

## Key Features
* **Passports:** 
    * **Official Passports:** Pre-loaded passports to track visits (National Parks, MLB Stadiums, Lego Stores, Buc-ee's).
    * **Personal Passports:** Full flexibility to create and edit custom tracking passports.
* **Standardized Views:**
    * **Map View:** A clean vector map using pins (for specific coordinates) or regions (for highlighting states/countries).
    * **List View:** A sortable, filtered list of locations categorized by "Visited" or "Planned."
    * **Passport View:** A digital "Stamp Book" layout organized into a grid system.
* **Data Privacy:** Designed for local deployment using Docker, keeping your location history on your own hardware.

I have a bad habit...

I start projects, get them to about 50% or 80% completion, and then let them sit in a random folder on my desktop until I forget how they work.

This site is my attempt to fix that.

This isn't just a portfolio; it’s a forcing function. By building a central hub, I’m forcing myself to actually finish and ship things. If a project is listed here, it means I am committing to finishing it.  Also, I am proud of the things I actually ship.

The secondary goal is to give back to the self-hosting community that I learned so much from. Hopefully, someone else will stumble upon the tools that make it to production here and find them useful.

### What to expect
You’re going to see a mix of things here:
* **Open Source Projects:** Tools I use daily, like my calendar and domain manager.
* **Ideas and Experiments:** Ideas that I am trying to turn into reality.
* **Sites:** Sites that I build that hopefully people find useful or helpful.
* **Blog Posts:** Little things like configs that I find useful or helpful. 
* **Life Stuff:** Websites I build for life journeys and travel challenges (like the JetBlue 25 for 25).

# A Privacy Focused App to Analyze Instagram Followers

> **Note:** This app is currently in production.

## Purpose
A privacy-focused tool designed to analyze your Instagram connections. It allows you to compare your "Followers" vs. "Following" lists to identify who isn't following you back, while also tracking follower trends (gains and losses) over time using historical data.

## Key Features
* **Snapshot Compare:** Analyze your current status by uploading your *followers* and *following* files to see:
    * Users you follow who don't follow you back.
    * Users following you who you don't follow back.
    * Mutual connections.
* **Trend Analysis:** Generate a timeline of exactly when you gained or lost specific followers by uploading multiple historical export files.
* **Privacy First:** All data processing happens **locally in your browser**. No data is ever sent to an external server.

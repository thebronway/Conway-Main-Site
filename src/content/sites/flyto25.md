## What is the jetBlue 25 for 25 Birthday Challenge?

In June 2025, jetBlue launched the **25 for 25 Challenge** to celebrate its 25th anniversary. The concept was simple but ambitious: fly to 25 different jetBlue destinations by December 31, 2025.

## The Rewards
Completing the challenge unlocks:
* **350,000 TrueBlue Points:** Worth approximately **$5,250** in flight value.
* **25 Years of Mosaic Elite Status:** Long-term premium benefits and perks.

## My Goal
Complete the challenge as quickly as possible while spending less than the total value of the points earned.

## Website Content
This site serves as an inside look at how I planned and executed the journey:

* **Detailed Itineraries:** A breakdown of every flight, hotel, train and leg of the journey.
* **Planning Spreadsheet:** My travel planning tool used to organize this.
* **Comprehensive Cost Breakdowns:** Detailed planned vs actual expenses for flights, hotels, trains, and car rentals.
* **Hacks & Tools:** The travel tips and flight-finding hacks that made the trip cost effecive and efficient.
* **Post-Trip Analysis:** Final results on total spend, lessons learned and fun stats.

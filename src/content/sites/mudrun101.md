# An OCR Mud Run Guide

![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-v0.0.0-blue.svg)

> **Note:** Development on this site is currently paused. I'm evaluating the niche audience to determine the best path forward and analyzing the project's longterm viability.

## Purpose
This site is designed to be a resource for first-timers and enthusiasts, providing advice for conquering obstacles and viewing historical data on race venues for OCR (Obstacle Course Racing) events like Spartan Race and Tough Mudder.

## Website Content
* **Venue Map Archive:** A historical collection of venue maps, allowing racers to analyze obstacle placement and terrain difficulty trends year-over-year.
* **Obstacles Guide & Tips:** Breakdowns of  obstacles with  techniques to overcome them.
* **Race Day Tips & Tricks:** Advice on gear selection, nutrition, and mental preparation to ensure you're ready for the starting line.
